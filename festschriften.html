166 entries
About this bibliography. Compiled independently beginning in 2023. A working bibliography of 166 entries + pending. A private Zotero database contains 187+ entries not yet fully cross-checked; reconciling the two and cross-checking with J. Periam Danton's Index to Festschriften in Librarianship (1970), Danton & Pulis (1979), and Otto Leitner's Internationale Bibliographie der Festschrifte, now the De Gruyter Internationale Jahresbibliographie der Festschriften (IJBF, 1980–present) is ongoing. Feedback welcome: entries missing from this list — especially from 1976–1979 and from Latin American, African, and Asian traditions — are actively sought. Contact: info at infophilia dot org
